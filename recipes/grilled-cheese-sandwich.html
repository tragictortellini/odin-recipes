Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Grilled Cheese Sandwich is a staple of the Sims games. So much so that in the Sims 4 you can unlock a hidden "Grilled Cheese" aspiration and become a Melt Master.

A grilled cheese sandwich is a comforting and easy recipe. Once you mastered it, you can get creative with your own picks and pairings. Delicious!

This recipe serves 4.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp. unsalted butter, softened
  • 8 slices of white sandwich bread
  • 4 slices of american cheese
  • 4 slices of medium cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat a large nonstick pan over medium heat.
  2. Add ½ Tbsp. butter. About 30 seconds later, when the butter starts to foam, swirl the pan to evenly coat it.
  3. Add 4 slices of bread. Top each one with 1 slice of american cheese and 1 of cheddar.
  4. Butter the remaining 4 slices of bread with the rest of the butter, then place them butter-side up on top of the cheese.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, untl each side is crisp and golden brown.
  6. To serve, transfer to 4 plates and let cool for 2 minutes before enjoying.
